HOLYMIND Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 March 2026

At HOLYMIND (“we”, “our”, “us”), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard information when you use our mobile applications (the “App”) and our marketing website at holymind.app (the “Site”).

1. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide to us
    Such as your email address or name if you contact us for support, sign up to a newsletter, or otherwise communicate with us.
  • Usage and device information
    When you use the Site or the App, we may automatically collect limited technical data, such as IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, app version, pages viewed, and general usage events (for example, which screens are opened and for how long).
  • App store and analytics data
    Apple App Store, Google Play and similar platforms may provide us with aggregated, anonymous analytics such as installs, uninstalls, crashes and country-level statistics.

2. How we use your information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide, maintain and improve the App and the Site.
  • Understand how people use HOLYMIND so we can make it more helpful and reliable.
  • Respond to your questions, requests and support enquiries.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services and prevent abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Legal bases (EEA & UK users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data only where we have a legal basis to do so, including:

  • To perform a contract with you (for example, to provide the App).
  • For our legitimate interests (for example, improving and protecting our services).
  • With your consent, where required (for example, optional communications).

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate, host, analyse or support our services (for example, analytics or error logging). These providers are only allowed to use your data to provide services to us and must protect it appropriately.
  • When required by law, regulation or legal process.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition or sale of all or part of our assets.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. We then delete or anonymise the information.

6. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information, such as:

  • Accessing the personal data we hold about you.
  • Requesting correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Objecting to or restricting certain processing.
  • Withdrawing consent where we rely on consent.

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details in the “Contact us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

7. Children’s privacy

HOLYMIND is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. International transfers

We may process and store information in countries outside of your own, where data protection laws may be different. When we transfer personal data internationally, we take appropriate steps to protect it in accordance with this Policy and applicable law.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top. If the changes are material, we may provide additional notice (for example, by highlighting the change in the App or on the Site).
